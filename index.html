See your storage like never before

Seven interactive views, three colour modes, and powerful tools to understand and reclaim your disk space.

🎨

7 Visualisations

Rings, treemap, pie chart, bubbles, table, histogram, and timeline views -- each revealing different insights about your files.

🔍

Duplicate Finder

Three-tier detection: Gold (exact SHA-256 match), Silver (same name + size), Bronze (same name). See exactly how much space is wasted.

🗑

Smart Cleanup

Automatically detects caches, temp files, old downloads, and duplicate apps. Preview savings before you commit.

📈

Storage Forecast

Tracks growth over time and projects when your drive will fill up. Daily and monthly growth rates at a glance.

👁

Watch Folder

Background monitoring with system notifications when folders grow significantly. Runs quietly in the system tray.

☁

Cloud Aware

Detects iCloud placeholders and online-only files. Filter between local, cloud, or all files instantly.

Seven ways to explore your files

Each visualisation mode reveals different patterns. Colour by age, type, or folder.

Rings

Concentric rings show folder hierarchy. Inner rings are top-level folders, outer rings drill deeper.

Treemap

Space-filling rectangles sized proportionally. The biggest files and folders are impossible to miss.

Bubbles

Circle packing with drill-down navigation. Click to dive into subfolders.

Pie Chart

Classic circular breakdown of your top files and folders by size.

Histogram

File size distribution across buckets from tiny (0-1 KB) to massive (10+ GB).

Timeline

Files grouped by modification date, from today to 5+ years ago.

More than just pretty charts

Tag files, stage deletions, export reports, and compare scans over time.

🏷

File Tags

Colour-coded Keep, Reviewed, and Delete tags to organise files as you go.

🗑

Collector

Staging area for batch deletions. Add files from any view, review, then delete in one go.

📄

Export

CSV file listing, full HTML report, or a tags report grouped by label.

🔄

Scan Comparison

Diff two scans to see what grew, what shrank, and what's new.

🌓

Dark & Light Themes

Toggle between dark and light mode with one click.

📂

Drag & Drop

Drop any folder onto the app to start scanning immediately.
